The Illustrated Book of Patience Games

Translated and Edited from German by Professor Hoffman, author of “Modern Magic”, “Drawing-Room Amusements” etc.

Professor Louis Hoffman was the nom de plume of Angelo John Lewis (1839–1919).

5th Edition

Originally published in 1904 by George Routledge & Sons Limited in London, England.

This work is therefore available in the public domain in both the US and UK as it was published prior to 1923.
